Recent Projects

Imocerto

Imocerto is a real estate web application built by a small team of friends to help users explore and evaluate properties across Portugal.

Although still in early development, the platform already enables users to browse listings and estimate renovation or construction costs with intuitive, user-friendly tools.

I developed the favorites system, allowing users to create custom property groups to organize and track listings of interest. This boosts engagement through personalized exploration.
